# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Overview

BuildIt does **not** use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

## What We Store Locally

BuildIt uses browser local storage and IndexedDB for essential application data:

- **Encryption keys**: Your cryptographic keys for E2EE communication
- **User preferences**: Language, theme, notification settings
- **Cached data**: Messages, contacts, and other data for offline access
- **Session state**: Application state for seamless user experience

## What We Do NOT Use

- No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- No advertising tracking
- No cross-site tracking
- No fingerprinting for tracking purposes

## Cloudflare

Our infrastructure runs on Cloudflare, which may set essential cookies for
security purposes (e.g., DDoS protection). These are not used for tracking.
See [Cloudflare's cookie policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/).
